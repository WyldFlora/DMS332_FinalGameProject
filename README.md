# DMS332_FinalGameProject

#Game Title: Trinket Trapper
#Pitch: Soar through the skies and collect all the shiny trinkets your heart desires!
#Audience and Genre: This is a laid back game focused on feeding the crow inside of all of #our brains. The genre is casual or cozy collector game, and is targeted towards those who 
#need something low energy but satisfying to fill their time.
#Synopsis: 
#Theme: Cozy, casual, fun! Fly around as a bird to collect all the shiny trinkets you #desire! 
#Goal: Gather trinkets to build your nest!
#Mechanics: Collect trinkets throughout the environment.
#Uniqueness: What other game are you able to play as a bird and collect shiny things? 
#Flowboard: Game Start: Wake up in your nest, you have a desire to collect!
#Step 1: Leave the nest, explore your environment. Has anything caught your eye?
#Step 2: Collect trinkets to your hearts content! Bring them back to your nest!
#Step 3: Can't find any more shiny trinkets? Try searching a new area!
